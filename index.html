Sam Altman

Entrepreneur, investor, and technology visionary. Sam Altman is the CEO of OpenAI and former president of Y Combinator, dedicated to ensuring that artificial general intelligence benefits all of humanity.

Key Achievements

Sam Altman has made significant contributions to the technology industry through his leadership, investments, and vision.

OpenAI

Co-founded OpenAI in 2015, a research laboratory with the mission to ensure that artificial general intelligence benefits all of humanity. Under his leadership, OpenAI has developed groundbreaking AI systems like GPT-4 and DALL-E.

Learn more →

Y Combinator

Served as the president of Y Combinator from 2014 to 2019, transforming it into the world's most influential startup accelerator. During his tenure, YC funded over 1,000 startups including Airbnb, Dropbox, and Stripe.

Investments & Ventures

A prolific investor with stakes in numerous successful companies including Stripe, Reddit, Airbnb, and many others. Founded Loopt (acquired in 2012) and has been involved in various ventures focused on advancing technology.
